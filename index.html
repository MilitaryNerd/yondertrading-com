About Yonder Trading

Yonder Trading LLC is a veteran-owned wholesale business based in Iowa, specializing in Amazon FBA partnerships with small and medium-sized businesses.

Our mission is to create mutually beneficial partnerships with established Amazon sellers. We understand the challenges of inventory management, cash flow, and scaling operations. That's why we offer a straightforward approach: we purchase wholesale inventory from your successful product lines and handle the FBA distribution, allowing you to focus on what you do best.
